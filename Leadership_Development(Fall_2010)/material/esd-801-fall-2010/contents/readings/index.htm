1 Introduction; distributed leadership framework  
2 Leadership lunch: Robert Liebeck

Augustine, Norman R. "Twelve Qualities of a Leader." Based on a presentation at The Colloquium on Leadership, Creativity and Values. The Center for Strategic and International Studies, Washington, DC, April 29, 1999.

Bennis, Warren, and Burt Nanus. "Mistaking Charge." Chapter 1 in Leaders: The Strategies for Taking Charge. 2nd ed. Harper Business, 2007. ISBN: 9780060559540

"Bennis' Leadership Qualities." ChangingMinds.org.
4 Discussion of readings; Apollo 13

Axelrod, Alan. "Queen of the Bottom Line." In Elizabeth I, CEO. Prentice Hall Press, 2002. ISBN: 9780735203570.

Shenk, Joshua Wolf. "The True Lincoln." TIME Magazine, July 2005, 39-44.

Drucker, Peter F. "The Discipline of Innovation." Chapter 7 in Leader to Leader. Edited by Frances Hesselbein and Paul M. Cohen. Jossey-Bass, 1999. ISBN: 9780787947262.

Zaleznik, Abraham. "Managers and Leaders: Are They Different?" In Designing and Managing Your Career. Edited by Harry Levinson. Harvard Business Review Press, 1988. ISBN: 9780875841809.

Bennis, Warren, and Burt Nanus. "Managers vs. Leaders." Chapter 2 in Leaders: The Strategies for Taking Charge. 2nd ed. Harper Business, 2007. ISBN: 9780060559540.

Gardner, Howard E. Multiple Intelligences. Basic Books, 1993. ISBN: 9780465018222.

Lowney, Chris. Heroic Leadership. Loyola Press, 2003. ISBN: 9780829418163
5 Leadership lunch: Brit d'Arbeloff  
6 Creativity and improvisation; discussion of assignments 3 and 4

Evans, Harold. "The Spark of Genius." US News, October 11, 2004.

"Tesla: Life and Legacy." Public Broadcasting Service, pbs.org.

"Edison's Miracle of Light." Public Broadcasting Service, pbs.org

Bennis, Warren, and Burt Nanus. "Taking Charge: Leadership and Empowerment." Chapter 7 in Leaders: The Strategies for Taking Charge. 2nd ed. Harper Business, 2007. ISBN: 9780060559540
7 Leadership lunch: Sharon Gillett  
8 Student oral presentations

Kaminski, Michelle. "Teaching Leadership to Union Women: The Use of Stories." Labor Studies Journal 28 (2003): 67-77.

Halpern, Belle Linda, and Kathy Lubar. "What Great Leaders Have." In Masters of Change. Edited by William Boast with Benjamin Martin. Executive Excellence Press, 1997. ISBN: 9781890009076.

Hill, Frederick W. "Leadership: A Personal Journey." Executive Speeches, February/March 2004, 5-9.

DiCicco, Joel M. "The Development of Leaders in Ancient China, Rome, and Persia." Public Administration Quarterly, Spring 2003, 6-40.

Alimo-Metcalfe, Beverly, and Robert J. Alban-Metcalfe. "The Development of a New Transformational Leadership Questionnaire." Journal of Occupational and Organization Psychology 74 (2001): 1-27.

Dingle, Derek T., and Alan Hughes. "A Time for Bold Leadership." Black Enterprises, February 2002, 76-82.

Kellerman, Barbara, and Scott W. Webster. "The Recent Literature on Public Leadership." The Leadership Quarterly 12 (2001): 485-514.

Ready, Douglas A. "How Storytelling Builds Next Generation Leaders." MIT Sloan Management Review, Summer 2002, 63-69.

House, Robert J., and Ram N. Aditya. "The Social Scientific Study of Leadership: Quo Vadis." Journal of Management 23, no. 3 (1997): 409-473.

Neureiter, Norman P. "Engineering and American Policy." The Bridge 34, no. 2 (2004).

Drath, Wilfred H. "Leading Together: Complex Challenges Require a New Approach." Leadership in Action 23, no. 1 (2003): 3-7.

LEGO® Simulation

Sessions 2-1, 2-3, and 2-5 comprise a Lego® enterprise simulation. In-class materials are not available on MIT OpenCourseWare, but the following paper and presentation describe the simulation and student reactions.

McManus, Hugh, Eric Rebentisch, Earll Murman, and Alexis Stanke. "Teaching Lean Thinking Principles Through Hands-On Simulations." Presented at the 3rd International CDIO Conference, MIT, June 11-14, 2007.

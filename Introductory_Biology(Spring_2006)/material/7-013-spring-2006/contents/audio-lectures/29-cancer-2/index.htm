So the embryo doesn't make it. So most of the time you cannot do that experiment. And, finally, I'd mentioned last time and I just want to emphasize that the genes that we've focused on for you in this class are genes that involve proliferation control and cell death.

And we'd mentioned briefly DNA damage control.

But there are many other processes that are important in tumorigenesis that are responsive to mutations in other kinds of genes like the process of invasion and angiogenesis and the all important process of metastasis.

Now, before I let you go, I want you think about the phenotypes. The phenotypes that you observe in these peas is smooth, in these peas is smooth and in these peas wrinkled.

So the ratio of phenotypes is 2:1.

You'll need to think about why those, sorry, sorry, two plus one is three, 3:1. You'll need to think about why those principles give rise to these calculations and these frequencies.
